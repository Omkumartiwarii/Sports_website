Musical Chair Event

Join us for an exciting game of Musical Chairs! The ultimate test of speed, attention, and reflexes. Can you outlast everyone and claim victory? Sign up now and show us your skills!

Event Rules

  • Walk around the circle of chairs as the music plays.
  • When the music stops, quickly find a chair to sit in.
  • One chair will be removed after each round, and the last person standing wins.
  • Stay within the designated play area.
  • Any form of cheating or pushing will result in disqualification.

Event Schedule

The event will follow this schedule:

  • Registration: 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Opening Ceremony: 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Musical Chairs Rounds: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Award Ceremony: 1:00 PM - 1:30 PM
