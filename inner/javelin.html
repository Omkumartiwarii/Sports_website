Javelin Event

Experience the thrill of the Javelin Throw! A true test of strength, skill, and precision. Ready to showcase your talent? Register now!

Event Rules

  • The thrower must stay behind the designated line while throwing the javelin.
  • Each athlete gets a maximum of 3 throws.
  • The longest throw wins.
  • Respect the designated safety zones and rules for the event.
  • No interference with other competitors.

Event Schedule

The event will follow this schedule:

  • Registration: 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM
  • Opening Ceremony: 10:30 AM - 11:00 AM
  • Javelin Throw: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Award Ceremony: 1:00 PM - 1:30 PM
